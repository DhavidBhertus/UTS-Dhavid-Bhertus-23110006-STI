Information System & Technology

The Information Systems and Technology (IST) study programme combines Information Systems (IS) and Information Technology (IT) to address the challenges of rapidly evolving technology and digital transformation. IST focuses on designing and implementing digital products (applications, websites, and designs) that align with business objectives and solve everyday life problems. It also explores emerging technologies.

Vision

Become an excellent study programme in the field of Information Systems & Technology, particularly in software engineering, and to contribute to the development of new technologies based on Christian values and a technopreneurial spirit.

Mission

  • Conducting education and teaching to produce high-quality graduates in the field of Information System & Technology that fulfill the needs of society and industry.
  • Conducting research to develop high-quality science & technology.
  • Organizing community service activities in the field of Information System & Technology.
  • Establishing cooperation between the Information System & Technology stude programme and relevant institutions & industries, both nationally and internationally.
  • Fostering the character of students by instilling Christian values, integrity, work ethic, and a sense of responsibility, thus equipping them to become succesful and contributing members of society.
