Institute Technology & Business Sabda Setia

Driven by a strong desire and passion to parcipate in the nation's aducational advancement process and as a continuation of the secondary education level organized by the Protestant Church Foundation of Kampung Bali (YGPKB), which is an extension of the sevice mandated by the West Kalimantan Christian Church (GKKB) Congregation of Pontianak in the field of education, and with the spirit of Indonesian nationalism and unity, on October 13th, 2019, the Higher Education Department (KomDikti) was formed by YGPKB Pontianak with the aim on establishing a higher education institution named the Institute of Technology and Business Sabda Setia (ITBSS).

Vision

A Higher Education Institution based on Christian values in preparing excellent human resources.

Mission

Educating and training human resources with character, skills, ready to compete globally , and bring positive impact to their surroundings.


Why Choose ITBSS?

ITBSS is very excited to welcome selected students to advance and grow together with ITBSS. Let's build a young intellectual community that will build the nation and the country. My Testimony

ITBSS has 3 undergraduate programs, such as System Information & Technology, Digital Business, and Enterpreneurship. This is my second semester at Information System & Technology programme as a student. First about the assignments, lecturers always give us so many assignments to do and some lectures give 2-3 assignments at the same time. Well, in my opinion, if these assignments are to increase our knowledge about the lessons then its fine. Next about the lessons, like the other universities in Pontianak, lecturers in here don't teach to much in class. Students must study by themself at home especially the students that have never study about Information System & Technology before. Last, there are so many courses related to the religion. Students always study about Christian religion every year in here and sometimes it feels like we are in Theology undergraduate programme. Anyway, ITBSS is Christian based college. So, you must get used to it when you are studying right here.
